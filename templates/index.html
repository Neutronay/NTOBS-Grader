How to Use ClassMark AI

1

Set Up Assessment Context

Enter the subject name, total max points, and optionally upload your Master Marking Scheme (.pdf or .txt) so the AI knows your grading criteria.

2

Configure Student Roster

Upload a class CSV/Excel file or set the number of students to automatically generate entry fields for student names.

3

Upload Scripts & Grade

Attach each student's PDF exam script to their respective name and hit process. The AI will evaluate each submission in parallel.

4

Review & Export

View full written feedback, download natively annotated PDFs with red grade marks on pages, or export the complete Excel gradebook.
